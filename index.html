Word Salad

This game will help you practice your understanding of the difference between a noun, an adjective, a verb and an adverb. We'll start with a few questions before building sentences with the words you have looked at.

Here is a reminder of the basics...

A noun (n)

is a...

An adjective (a)

is a...

A verb (v)

is a...

An adverb (av)

is a...

For example, can you see where the noun is, where the adjective is and where the verb is in the following sentence?

The black cat runs quickly.

(n) cat

(a) black

(v) runs

(av) quickly
